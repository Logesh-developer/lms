Sick Leave Policy

Introduction:

At LM solutions, we understand that unexpected illness can happen to anyone. We believe in supporting our employees through challenging times and fostering a healthy work-life balance. This policy outlines the guidelines for utilizing sick leave in a fair and consistent manner.

Eligibility:

All full-time and part-time employees are eligible to accrue and use sick leave.

Accrual:

Full-time employees accrue 30 hours of sick leave per period ([e.g., month, quarter]). Part-time employees accrue 30 hours of sick leave per [W] period ([e.g., week, month]).

Leave Usage:

Sick leave can be used for absences due to:

  • Personal illness or injury.
  • Caring for a sick family member (e.g., spouse, child, parent).
  • Attending medical appointments.

Employees are required to notify their manager as soon as possible when they are unable to work due to illness. This notification should be made through [preferred method, e.g., email, phone call].

For absences exceeding 3 consecutive days, a doctor's note may be required.

Documentation:

Employees may be required to provide documentation supporting their sick leave usage, such as a doctor's note or other medical evidence.

Paid Leave:

sick leave is paid only if it exceeds 7 days

Misuse of Leave:

Misuse of sick leave, such as providing false or misleading information about illness, may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

Additional Resources:

Employees who require additional support or have questions about the sick leave policy should contact [HR department, manager, etc.].
